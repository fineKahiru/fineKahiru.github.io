RoadMap

DISTRICT 1 - Heroes of Ruy
 
Heroes of Ruy: Genesis collection launch. 5 factions distributed among 7.222 pieces.
Reveal day: International DJs coming straight from Ultra music Festival and Tomorrowland, such as Matt Nash and Different Heaven will join the community on this momentus occasion.

DISTRICT 2 - The Way

Citizens Merit: Holders will be able to redeem and store their first Manuscript after holding period.
Stash: Genesis holders will be rewarded with benefits. Get Stone Particles [an off-chain token] to claim rewards from partnerships and whitelist opportunities. Gradually accumulate them to be exchanged for different items.
First Prophecy: Holders will be able use the Manuscript to redeem 1 x Digital Comic Cover that will work as a “pass” to access our DAO.
DAO infrastructure: Opportunities to voice and contribute to the development and the future of Kahiru on a decentralized basis.
The bridge: In real life cooperations. The digital and physical world come together.

DISTRICT 3 - The Expansion

Divine Manuscript: 1 x Physical Kahiru Comic will become available and claimable to every Kahiru Genesis + Cover holders.
